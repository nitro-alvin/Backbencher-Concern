Main Description

Backbencher Concern is a YouTube channel dedicated to celebrating the vibrant culture of Kerala’s private buses, with a special focus on the iconic Anna Mariya. This channel is a tribute to the lifeline of Kerala’s roads, where every bus is not just a mode of transport but a moving canvas of creativity, energy, and stories.

The channel dives deep into the world of private buses, showcasing their unique designs, mesmerizing artwork, thunderous sound systems, and the unforgettable experiences they create for their passengers—especially those cherished backbenchers. From the roaring engines to the dynamic driving styles, every element of Kerala’s private bus culture is brought to life here.

Anna Mariya, the star of our channel, is not just a bus but a symbol of the vibrant lifestyle and traditions that these buses represent. We bring you exclusive behind-the-scenes looks at the craftsmanship that goes into designing these buses, interviews with drivers and conductors, and stories of the people who make these buses legendary.

Whether you’re a bus enthusiast, a fan of Kerala’s unique cultural elements, or someone looking for a nostalgic trip down memory lane, Backbencher Concern has something for everyone. Join us on this colorful journey as we explore the roads of Kerala, one thrilling ride at a time!

About Backbencher Concern

Welcome to Backbencher Concern, your one-stop destination for celebrating the vibrant and dynamic world of Kerala’s private bus culture. Our website is dedicated to exploring the charm, artistry, and stories behind Kerala’s most iconic private buses, with a special spotlight on the legendary Anna Mariya.

Here, you’ll find a treasure trove of content ranging from detailed articles, behind-the-scenes features, and exclusive interviews with the people who bring these buses to life. Dive into our galleries of vibrant images that capture the stunning designs, artistic expressions, and everyday adventures of these moving marvels.

Whether you're a fan of Kerala's unique bus culture, an enthusiast who loves the roar of engines, or just curious about the stories these buses hold, Backbencher Concern is here to bring you closer to the heart of Kerala's roads. Explore, connect, and ride along with us as we celebrate the spirit of the backbenchers!
